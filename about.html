ABOUT US

The Company is committed to the provision of various Information Technology services such as Computer Hardware, Maintenance and software support services for Micro Computers, Servers and Peripheral systems in use at Government departments, Local Government authorities, Land Boards, Non-Government Organizations, Parasternal organizations, big corporate entities and Private companies. We also provide Hardware sales, software sales, consultancy, computer training, computer repair and maintenance, Network solutions, installations and configurations etc. The key personnel in the organization are sound specialists in the ICT, Educational and Office Hardware Solutions. All hardware go through rigorous quality control (QC) and quality assurance (QA) checks before being dispatched. All equipment meets and exceeds ISO 9001/9002:2000 and are BOBS certified for local use. We stock P500, 000+ of branded spare Parts, printers, computers and other hardware for fast resolution and replacement on faulty equipment reported to our helpdesk at any given time. Our response and resolution times for support services are usually within 30minutes to 2 hours in Gaborone and surrounding areas. We are pleased to have a track record of providing a stable I.T environment to all the clients we are supporting.
